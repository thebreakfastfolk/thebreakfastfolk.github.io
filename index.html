The Breakfast Folk

The Breakfast Folk is an opportunity for anyone anywhere to participate in live folk music.
The idea came from a group of North-east folk musicians who due to family commitments were unable to regularly attend the more traditional evening pub-based sessions.
The saturday morning timing is therefore aimed at enabling young families and beginner musicians to play in a friendly environment and experience and develop playing live folk music.

  • 1 hour folk music session at 9.30 am every Saturday
  • Free to attend
  • Any instrument welcome
  • For musicians of all ages and all abilities
  • Sheet music for the tunes being played will be made available when possible
  • Families, friends and relatives all welcome to enjoy listening, have a cup of tea, have a chat, etc
  • Spacious venues to allow room for toddlers and babies to have some fun
